Baked Ziti

Description

A recipe so good that you will have to call your mother to tell her that her cooking sucks!

Ingredients

  • Dry Ziti
  • Pasta sauce
  • Mozzerella
  • Provolone
  • Sour cream
  • Parmesean
  • Ground beef
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Boil pasta and preheat oven
  2. Cook ground beef and dice onions
  3. Add onions to beef
  4. Add pasta sauce to beef and onions after beef has browned
  5. Simmer sauce for 10 minutes on low-med heat
  6. Layer the dish in order
    1. Half ziti
    2. Mozzerella
    3. Sour cream
    4. Half pasta sauce
    5. Half ziti
    6. Provolone
    7. Half pasta sauce
  7. Bake in oven at 350 farenheight for 30 mim
  8. Let cool and serve fresh
