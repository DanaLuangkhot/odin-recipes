Whipped Cream

Description

Have you ever wanted whipped cream but were too good to drive to the store and buy a can of Reddi Wip like everyone else? Boy, do i have the recipe for you>

Ingredients

  • Heavy whipping cream
  • Confectioner's sugar
  • Vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Whip heaving whipping cream until frothy
  2. Gradually add sugar and vanilla extract
  3. Continue mixxing until stiff peaks form
  4. Becareful not to over mix to prevent curdling
  5. Serve chilled
